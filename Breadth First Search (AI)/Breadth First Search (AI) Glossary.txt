Queue - Data structure that stores items like a line at the store. The first item on, is the first item out.
Dequeue - Take off the item at the top of the queue. This is the item that has been there the longest.
Enqueue - Place the item on the queue. The item will be put on the back of the queue.
Visited - Set of nodes that have been examined.
Neighbors - Nodes that have a direct path to one another.
Goal - Node the algorithm is looking for.
